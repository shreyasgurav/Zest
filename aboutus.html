WTF is Zest?

Well, we don’t know either. I just thought, do social media apps help us to be connected? or just create an illusion that you are living the life? Social Media was a revolutionary product that changed the way of communication, but the addiction of this could destroy your social life. Zest is an Event Hosting Platform and Social Media combined, which allows event hosters to list their event and sell their tickets through Zest. Users can attend events, and meet new peoples as Zest allow users to check who all are attending that show.
