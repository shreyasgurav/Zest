Top Go-Karting Tracks in Mumbai

Experience the thrill of speed at Mumbai's premier go-karting destinations! Whether you're a racing enthusiast or looking for an exciting weekend activity, these top-rated karting tracks offer state-of-the-art facilities, safety equipment, and unforgettable racing experiences for all skill levels.

#1 Top Rated

Ajmera I-Land Go-Karting

Wadala, Mumbai

Mumbai's largest go-karting track featuring a 600-meter international standard circuit. Perfect for both beginners and experienced racers with professional-grade karts and timing systems.

₹500 per race 600m track Open daily
#2

Hakone Go-Karting

Malad West, Mumbai

Indoor karting facility with electric go-karts and a multi-level track design. Features advanced safety systems and is perfect for corporate events and birthday parties.

₹450 per race Indoor track Electric karts
#3

Smaaash Go-Karting

Lower Parel, Mumbai

Part of the popular Smaaash entertainment center, offering a compact but thrilling track experience along with other gaming activities under one roof.

₹600 per race VR racing available Combo packages
#4

KartZone Racing

Andheri East, Mumbai

Professional karting circuit with high-speed straights and challenging corners. Offers racing leagues and tournaments for competitive racers.

₹550 per race Racing leagues Pro karts available
#5

Raceway Karting

Powai, Mumbai

Family-friendly karting venue with separate tracks for kids and adults. Great for weekend outings with special packages for groups and families.

₹400 per race Kids track Family packages
